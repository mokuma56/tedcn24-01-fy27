# Section 4: Team Challenge - Agentic Ops

In this section, teams will compete in two hands-on challenges that put their AI and agentic skills to the test. First, teams will engineer the most effective AI Canvas prompts to generate a comprehensive Root Cause Analysis for a real network or security scenario, then iterate, compare, and present their approach to a proctor. Second, teams will design and build a fully functional AI agent using Agent Builder in Cisco Cloud Control that solves a real operational problem and can be demonstrated live to a customer.

!!! team-challenge "🏆 Team Challenge — AI Canvas RCA"
    Work as a team to craft the most effective AI Canvas prompts for your assigned lab pod scenario. Your goal is to engineer a prompt — or a sequence of prompts — that produces a comprehensive **Root Cause Analysis (RCA)** for a real network or security issue relevant to your environment.

    A strong RCA should clearly identify:

    - The **problem statement** — what is happening and where

    - **Contributing factors** — conditions that led to or worsened the issue

    - **Root cause(s)** — the underlying reason the issue occurred

    - **Impact assessment** — what is affected and how severely

    - **Recommended remediation steps** — clear, actionable next steps

    As a team, iterate on your prompts, compare outputs, and agree on the approach that produces the clearest and most complete RCA. Use the **Appendix A: Sample AI Prompts** as a starting point — build on them, combine them, or go beyond them entirely.

    Once your team has agreed on the best-performing prompt and validated the quality of its output, nominate a spokesperson to **present your findings to your assigned proctor**. Be prepared to walk through:

    - The prompt(s) you crafted and any iterations you made along the way

    - Why your team selected this approach over other candidates

    - The RCA output it produced and how it applies to your lab pod scenario

    - How you would use this in a real customer demonstration

    Proctors will evaluate each team on **prompt quality, RCA completeness, and clarity of presentation**. The team that demonstrates the most effective and well-reasoned approach will be recognized as the **winning team**. Good luck! 🏆

!!! team-challenge "🏆 Team Challenge — Agent Builder"
    Now it is time to put your Agent Builder skills to the ultimate test. As a team, design and build the **most creative, useful, and impressive AI agent** you can using Agent Builder in Cisco Cloud Control.

    Your agent should solve a real operational problem relevant to your lab environment. There are no restrictions on the use case — the only requirement is that it must be functional, demonstrable, and something you could confidently show to a customer. Think about what makes an agent truly valuable: the tools it has access to, the context it understands, and the outcomes it can drive autonomously.

    Consider the following as inspiration:

    - An agent that monitors for anomalies and automatically triggers a diagnostic workflow

    - An agent that integrates ServiceNow to create and resolve tickets based on real-time infrastructure events

    - An agent that performs a cross-domain health check across Meraki, Secure Access, and Catalyst SD-WAN

    - An agent that guides a junior engineer through a remediation process step by step

    - Or something entirely your own — the more creative the better

    When your agent is ready, nominate a spokesperson and **demonstrate it live to your assigned proctor**. Your presentation should cover:

    - The **problem or use case** your agent addresses

    - How the agent is **configured** — tools, context, triggers, and behavior

    - A **live demonstration** showing the agent in action

    - The **business value** — why a customer would want this agent and what outcome it delivers

    Proctors will evaluate each team on **creativity, technical execution, and quality of the customer-facing narrative**. The team with the most compelling and well-executed agent will be crowned the **Agent Builder Champions**. Good luck! 🏆
